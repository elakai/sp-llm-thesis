## 2_POLICY_AND_RULE_CONFLICTS
**User:** Am I allowed to wear shorts and slippers inside the Cisco Lab?

**AXIsstant:**
According to the CISCOLab Operations Manual, students are required to always wear closed shoes when performing an experiment. However, it does not explicitly state that shorts are not allowed. On the other hand, the 2025 ADNU COLLEGE HANDBOOK states that knee-length walking shorts are allowed for both male and female students. As for slippers, the handbook prohibits rubber slippers, but it does not mention other types of slippers. To be safe, it's best to wear closed shoes and avoid wearing slippers inside the Cisco Lab.

> **Source certainty:** High — based on 14 document(s): *2025 Adnu College Handbook   Chapter 9.1.2, 2025 Adnu College Handbook   Chapter 7*
